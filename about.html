About the Placement Portal

The Placement Portal is a web-based application designed to streamline campus placement activities at SSSIHL. It allows students, faculty, and administrators to access role-specific dashboards and manage placement-related tasks.

Features

  • Secure login via Google Sign-In
  • Role-based dashboard routing
  • Integration with Google Sheets for student data
  • Faculty and admin tools for placement coordination

Data Usage

We only use your Google email address to identify your role. No other personal data is accessed or stored.
