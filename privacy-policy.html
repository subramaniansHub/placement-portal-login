Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how the Placement Portal uses Google user data.

What We Collect

We collect your Google email address after you sign in using Google OAuth. No other personal data is accessed or stored.

How We Use It

Your email is used solely to identify your role (student, faculty, admin) and route you to the appropriate dashboard. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or profiling.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties.

Data Retention

We do not store your email address beyond the duration of your session. No persistent storage is used.

Security

All data is transmitted securely using HTTPS. We follow best practices to protect user information.

Compliance

This app’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
